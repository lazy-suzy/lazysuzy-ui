How does your order process work?
The LazySuzy marketplace helps you order from several home brands through a single checkout process and one total payment. We then work with our retailer and brand partners to coordinate shipping and logistics to make sure you have the best possible post-purchase experience. Not only do you save yourself the pain of having to repeat the same ordering process across several sites, you can also be rest assured that we are working hard on your behalf to receive priority service and contininuous communication on your order status.
What We Offer
  • A single platform to search and browse home furniture of differentiated quality, function and design with the best possible prices we can source for you
  • Single order process for all furniture in your Shopping Bag from our sellers, with one quick checkout
  • Price Match! If you find a lower price anywhere else on an identical product, we'll gladly refund the difference
  • Access to any valid seller promotion codes as well as additional exclusive promotions
  • Communicate with all of your sellers and keep you updated as items ship and/or are ready to be scheduled for delivery
  • Liaise with brands and retailers directly on returns
How are products ordered on our site and shipped to you?
Generally, we don't hold any inventory ourselves and ship products directly from sellers once you place an order. This helps keep costs down and offer you the best possible price on all items listed on our site. In some cases, where items are selling quickly and are at risk of being sold out, we may purchase and hold stock directly so that we can offer our users the opportunity to access such items exclusively. Look for the *Exclusive* tage on products that we offer for online ordering that may not be available anywhere else.
How are we able to offer products at prices lower than the seller's own listed price?
Through our marketplace program, we're able to access product at preferred pricing and through our super low cost operations we are able to pass on a bulk of the savings to our customers.
What is our shipping policy?
In most cases, we follow the shipping rates offered by sellers and generally includes one flat fee for all bulk items ordered from sellers for white-glove in home delivery and assembly.
We are working towards shifting our site towards 100% free delivery and are gradually working towards that goal. Bear with us while we keep working to bring you an ever improving experience!
What happens if an item you order is out of stock or facing delayed delivery times?
In most cases, if there is any issue with an item your ordered that requires your attention, we will reach out on the same business day - no matter the time! - and at the latest by the following day. If an item is completely sold out, we will refund your credit card immediately and if there are unexpected delays we will work with you to proceed with the approach that suits you best.
Can items ordered from multiple sellers be delivered on the same day?
For in-home delivery, we can certainly work with you to help coordinate delivery of items from multiple sellers to arrive on the same day. We know the commitment it is to set up a home with new furnishings and will aim to make the experience as smooth as possible for you.
Can you place orders to International locations outside of the United States?
At this time, we are unable to serve international locations. If you are based in Canada, send us a note and we can try to work with you to accomodate your needs.
How are returns handled?
For most items listed on our site, we accept returns in accordance the seller's return policy. Most sellers offer favorable return policies except for clearance items which are generally sold as final and indicated by prices ending in .99 or .98.
Certain items sold directly by us through our own shipping service are also sold as final sales. This helps us able to offer competitive shipping rates on items that are otherwise not available for online ordering by sellers directly and makes items accessible nationwide. In some cases, returns may also be accepted for drop off at a seller's nearest location to you.
With that said, if you are ever unhappy with your purchase please reach out to us and we will do everything we can to find a solution that works for you.
